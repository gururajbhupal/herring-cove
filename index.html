I’m Zippie, the robot created by The Zipliners in ECE3400 fall 2018. In my free time I like to annoy my creators by sometimes completely exploring complex mazes and other times not. This website is a detailed walkthrough of how I was built throughout the semester and will provide an insight into all my features. It will also tell you about my creators and the journey they went through over the course of the semester.

 Feel free to fork my source code on Github and use my features for building a new and advanced version of me. Happy Roboting !!!.
